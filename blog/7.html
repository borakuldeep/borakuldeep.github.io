A 3D design for modern hipster camera (3D wireframe)

A portable smart camera v 1.0

My attempt at a fun 3D design project

Problem statement

As a camera enthusiast and a photographer, I always wanted latest gears. Even though the upgrades are marginal often. I am a big fan of small and portable camera with no interchangeable lens, yet with advantages of several lenses, similar to smartphones of today. So, my ideal camera would be very similar to smartphones but with bigger sensors and dedictaed functionality of photography. What would that camera look like? This is my take on that.

User requirements

  • Smaller form factor
  • Great image quality
  • Professional video features
  • Ease of use
  • cheaper price (~$1000)
  • Great looking design (subjective)

Design

The designs are based on google pixel original phone as I was big fan of it's camera system.

wireframe - Camera front

  • This is the camera front / top view.
  • The camera is inspired by smartphones.
  • The camera has multiple lens for different shooting situations. These sensors should be larger than a typical smartphones.

wireframe - Camera bottom side

  • This is the view from side-bottom.
  • The bottom of camera has a tripod mount and battery container. Minimalist design :)
  • A good amount of hand grip needed for better handling. On second thought, the grip can be used to hold battery just like other cameras. This will allow a bigger battery.

wireframe - Camera side angle

  • This view shows headphone / mic ports and SD card slot.
  • Also present is usb-c and mini micro ports for charing and data transfer. Micro usb port is probably not needed.

wireframe - Camera back

  • This is the camera back view
  • The camera contains a big touch screen, around 5.5 inches.
  • The software interface is task for another day but I am assuming some form of android OS, given it's based on Google Pixel.
  • It features speakers / inbuilt micrphones as well.
  • Missing is the hotshoe mount for attaching external accessories. But I assume bluetooth and NFC might take care of that.

wireframe - Camera back closeup

  • This is the camera back closeup view.
  • It just shows the top plate in more details.

And that's it. This was a fun project and my first time hands on with a 3D design app on iPad. This design is not industrial ready by any means. Just a draft 3D wireframe if you will :) . Thanks for viewing.
