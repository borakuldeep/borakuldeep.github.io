App for tracking food delivery (UI screen)

Delivery Tracking App v 1.0

A food delivery tracking app for restaurants

Problem statement

As a restaurant owner, I want to to able to get new orders and track their delivery status on a single platform, so that I can better manage my otherwise messy delivery management console.

User requirements

The app should allow a restaurant owner:

  • View new orders and see status of orders
  • View the order location on a map.
  • Able to assign orders to derivers once they are ready to go.
  • Track drivers and estimated time delivery.

UI screens

Dashboard / Home screen

This is the admin dashboard, where an admin / user can view and track orders.



A lots of things going on in this screen. Below is the breakdown of few things.

  • The new orders panel on left side is where all new orders will arrive. The colors are indicative of how long they are waiting there for.
  • ORDER DETAILS - On clicking an order, a new pop up will show details about the order.


  • NEW ORDER - The new odrer will be displayed in bright red until it's ready and assigned to a driver


  • DRAG n DROP - Once the order is cooked and ready to go, admin can drag and drop the order to panel below that - ready to go.


  • ASSIGN DRIVERS - The drivers should be able to pick up ready to go orders from their mobile app.
  • User can click the order location and get more info about it.


  • ORDER TRACKING - The driver location and tracking info is also visible for quick time estimate. Driver's info and location should be communicated via the mobile app.


  • ORDER COMPLETE - The delivered orders and assigned drivers are shown in lighter color. Once the driver arrives back at restaurant, they will be available in panel. The finished order location will be removed from UI after say 2 min from delivery time.


  • QUICK INFO - On hovering to orders and drivers, admin should be able to see quick details..


  • DARK MODE - And there is a dark mode as always. This can be toggled via theme switcher buttton on bottom right.


This is a work in progress. Some other screens needs to be added and designs will be improved.
